I am an assistant professor in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Lehigh University. Before joining Lehigh, I work at AMD Research during 2015-2020, where I worked on FastForward 2 and PathForward Programs funded by the Department of Energy, aiming to accelerate the R&D necessary to build the nationâ€™s first exascale supercomputers.
